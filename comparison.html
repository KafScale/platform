Notes

  • *Cost estimates are approximate for 100GB/day ingestion, 7-day retention, 3 brokers. Actual costs vary by region, traffic patterns, and configuration.
  • WarpStream was acquired by Confluent (IBM) in September 2024. Future direction uncertain.
  • BSL licenses (Business Source License) restrict commercial use as a managed service without a separate agreement.
  • Tiered storage offloads cold data to S3 but still requires local disks for hot data and replication.
  • S3-only storage means all data (hot and cold) is stored in object storage, eliminating disk management entirely.
  • KafScale intentionally omits transactions and compacted topics to optimize for ETL, log aggregation, and async event workloads.
